About me

Born and raised in a remote city in Southern Italy, after High School I decided to leave my almost twelve thousand fellow villagers to move to Milan and then to London, to study Visual Design and to challenge myself.
I have always cultivated a passion for literature and writing is my cure against all evils.
I have many interests in different fields, such as photography, philosophy, DIY, illustration, animation.
Music is my real lifeblood, I listen to it continuously and I always like to discover new artists, with a preference for rock and its derivatives.
I haven’t figured out yet what I like the most between analogic and digital, so you can find me either doing some etching in a printmaking studio, or in a library designing a website.
My ambition is to visit as many places as possible, and to keep learning and having fun with what I’m passionate about, in the field of visual design.

If you'd like to know what I use to create, feel free to visit my kitchen.
