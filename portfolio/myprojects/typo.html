First Thing First

Layout of a booklet containing the script of the “First Things First” manifesto.
This project contemplated the mandatory selection and the usage of just one font style, always to be applied with the same weight. In this case, the selected font of choice was Futura Light. With the mandatory use of just one font and weight, the body was the only variant that could be used in order to make the work more dynamic.

Specimen

Creation of a booklet containing the specimen of a chosen font.
The character chosen was Skolar Sans by David Březina and Sláva Jevčinová, and all the aspects and potentialities have been investigated, through the use of different bodies, weights and styles. In order to give a complete overview, all the glyphs of the font itself, the ligatures, and the supported languages were also investigated.
