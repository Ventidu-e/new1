Ex Designers

Group project consisted of choosing a movie opening, reinterpreteting it and adapting it to new technical/narrative needs. We wrote a new script, chose some locations and we made a new video, which involved the members of the group both as actors and also as videomakers. The sequence reinterpreted is the initial scene of the movie "Ex Drummer".
